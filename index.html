What About Me?

Welcome to my website! So what about me? Let me explain who I am and my background. During my time at Arizona State University, I majored in Communication and Media Analysis thinking I wanted to be in Public Relations. During my last year at ASU, I was introduced to the Arduino and C++. I was amazed at what technology can do! I found my passion! From there I switched gears and decided to pursue coding. I packed my bags and enrolled in a coding bootcamp in San Jose called Coding Dojo learning full stack development and mobile. After I graduated, I didn't stop.
